About Us

  • About Us

We are a real estate brokerage, development, and investment advisory firm domiciled in Ikoyi, the most high-brow neighborhood in Lagos, Nigeria, with highly experienced affiliates in neighboring cities. At Centania homes, we see a home as the most valuable investment, and the greatest necessity in the world of man.

We believe that every income earner, irrespective of their varying budgets, should own a home which meets the increasing demand of the modern-day urban architecture – a home embedded in luxury. Consequently, we make the process of buying, selling, and rehabilitating homes for our clients hassle-free.

In clear terms, the following activities summarize our organizational approach.

  • We Buy: acquire properties for our clients in desirable neighborhoods.
  • We Sell: Smooth property sales across Lagos and beyond
  • We Rent: We rent properties to low-income and high-income families in safe, desirable areas
  • We Rehabilitate: We ransform “ugly” houses into beautiful homes
  • We Finance: Our real estate finance practice focuses on the financing and refinancing of the sale, purchase, development and management of real estate assets of all types. our real estate finance team is diverse, with a culture of technical excellence on loan financings carefully built over the years.
  • We advise: A huge pressure is being mounted on the real estate sector across the globe, as rapid urbanization and the quest for re-urbanization are driving the business forward for investment cycles and capital market movements. consequently, the task of improving the performance of real estate, and offering service value is becoming more demanding.
